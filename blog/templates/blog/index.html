Featured Book: The Neglected One

There are moments in life when we sense that something vital is missing—not from the world around us, but from within us.

The Neglected One is a heartfelt reflection on rediscovering the quiet, transforming Presence of the Holy Spirit—the One who has been with us all along, waiting not to be understood, but recognized and embraced. This book is not written to instruct, but to awaken—to call us back to the stillness where faith breathes again.
